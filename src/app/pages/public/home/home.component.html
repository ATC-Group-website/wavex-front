What is WaveX...?

WaveX is a high intensity, low impact workout performed on a specialised floating platform, challenging your balance, strength and endurance like never before. The unstable surface amplifies every movement, engaging your core and stabilizer muscles for a full-body burn.

What is WaveX Sport?

WaveX Sport is an innovative fitness experience that takes your workout to the water-literally. Using our signature floating WaveX mats, all exercises are performed while balancing on water, turning every move into a full-body challenge. The natural instability of the water engages your core, sharpens your focus, and amplifies the benefits of traditional workouts, all while keeping it low-impact and fun.
