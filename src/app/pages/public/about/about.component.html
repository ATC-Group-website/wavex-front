What is Wavex .. ?

WaveX is a high intensity, low impact workout performed on a specialised floating platform, challenging your balance, strength and endurance like never before. The unstable surface amplifies every movement, engaging your core and stabilizer muscles for a full-body burn.

What Drives Us

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of innovative aquatic fitness experiences by making water-based training more accessible, effective, and enjoyable. We aim to inspire people of all ages and fitness levels to move with confidence, connect through community, and discover new possibilities for health and performance both on and off the water.

Our Mission

At WaveX Sport, our mission is to deliver a fun, transformative, and inclusive workout experience through water-based fitness. We aim to empower individuals of all fitness levels to improve their strength, balance, and overall wellness by introducing unique, floating-mat exercises that challenge the body and energize the mind.

History ... !

The concept of performing structured fitness workouts on water originated in the early 2010s, with the fusion of stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) and traditional studio workouts like yoga, Pilates, and core training. Initially popularized along coastal wellness retreats in California and Australia, this innovative form of exercise quickly evolved into a structured sport.
