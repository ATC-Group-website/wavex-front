What is Wavex .. ?

WaveX is a high intensity, low impact workout performed on a specialised floating platform, challenging your balance, strength and endurance like never before. The unstable surface amplifies every movement, engaging your core and stabilizer muscles for a full-body burn.

History ... !

The concept of performing structured fitness workouts on water originated in the early 2010s, with the fusion of stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) and traditional studio workouts like yoga, Pilates, and core training. Initially popularized along coastal wellness retreats in California and Australia, this innovative form of exercise quickly evolved into a structured sport known as "Wave X Board Fitness" or "FloatFit".
